What We Do
 
aClaim is a leader in the development of online, point-of-service telecommunications technology for the pharmacy sector of the healtcare industry. Utilizing proprietary, custom-designed and developed software, aClaim adjudicates prescription drug claims at speeds unmatched in the industry, and in compliance with all standards set by the Nation Council of Prescription Drug Programs (NCPDP) and the Health Insurance Portability and Accounting Act (HIPAA).

aClaim's core strengths are developing claims processing functionality for partners that are not traditional insurance-based businesses. aClaim builds custom solutions for organizations that need the efficiency of automated, real-time prescription claims processing without the restraints inherent to other claims processing systems.

aClaim is a unique boutique pharmacy benefit manager, capable of quickly implementing custom solutions for our customers. Our motto is "If you can dream it, aClaim can process it!"
